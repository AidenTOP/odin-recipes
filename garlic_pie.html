Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Pie crust
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Roast garlic for 8 hours
  3. Lay evenly in pie crust
  4. Add 2 cups milk
  5. Cook for 20 minutes
  6. Eat
